# Graphing 3D Shapes

## Elipsoid
$$(\frac{x}{a})^1 + (\frac{y}{b})^2 +(\frac{z}{c})^2=1$$All traces of an ellipsoid are either ellipses or single points. 

## Parabaloid
**Elliptic:**
$$z = (\frac{x}{a})^2+(\frac{y}{b})^2$$
**Hyperbolic:**
$$z = (\frac{x}{a})^2-(\frac{y}{b})^2$$
