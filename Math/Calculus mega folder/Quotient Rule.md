This is one of the [[Derivatives|derivative]] rule that lets us solve derivatives where we have two functions on top of each other, for example:
$$H(x) = \frac{f(x)}{g(x)}$$
and how we can handle that is by applying the formula
$$H'(x)=\frac{f'(x)g(x)-f(x)g'(x)}{(g(x))^2}$$
