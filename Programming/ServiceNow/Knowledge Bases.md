
[[ServiceNow]]

This is a way to provide knowledge articles to users or employees so that information on certain processes, trouble shooting, etc is easy to use.

**Knowledge Management**
Knowledge Management allows users to create, categorize, review, approve, and browse important information in a way that is shared by the entire organization.

**User Criteria**
This is a way that allows users to only see articles that are relevant to them. These conditions are evaluated against users to determine which users can read, write, create, or retire articles. Localization also exists for people in other countries who may speak other languages.
