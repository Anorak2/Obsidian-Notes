# Cross product
to find the cross product of two [[Vector Definition|vectors]], say $\lt a, b, c \gt$ cross $\lt u, v, w \gt$ you would start by forming a [[Matrix Definition|matrix]] where the first row is set to i, j, k, the second row is the first vector and the third row is the second vector.

$$ \begin{bmatrix}
i & j & k \\
a & b & c \\
u & v & w \\
\end{bmatrix} $$
you then find the [[Determinant of 2x2 Matrix|determinant]] of this matrix using the formula for [[Determinants with Cofactor expansion|cofactor expansion]]
$$ i\begin{bmatrix}
b & c \\
v & w \\
\end{bmatrix} - j\begin{bmatrix}
a & c \\
u & w \\
\end{bmatrix}+k \begin{bmatrix}
a & b \\
u & v \\
\end{bmatrix} $$
