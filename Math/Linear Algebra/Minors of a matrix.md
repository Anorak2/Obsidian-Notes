You find the $a_{ij}$ minor of a matrix by "deleting" the i and jth row and then calculating the [[determinant properties|determinant]] of that matrix
$$
\begin{bmatrix}
a & b & c \\
d & e & f \\
g & h & i \\
\end{bmatrix}
$$
$$a_{2,1} = det\begin{bmatrix}
b & c \\
h & i \\
\end{bmatrix} $$
The minor of a matrix will always have a size of n-1
