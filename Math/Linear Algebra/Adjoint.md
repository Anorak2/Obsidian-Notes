The adjoint matrix of A is defined to be the transpose $C^T$ of the [[Co-factors of a Matrix|co-factor matrix]] C

$$Adj(A)=C^T=\begin{bmatrix}
C_{11} & C_{21} & ... & C_{n1} \\
C_{12} & C_{22} & ... & C_{n2} \\
... & ... & ... & ... \\
C_{1n} & C_{21} & ... & C_{nn} \\
\end{bmatrix}$$
