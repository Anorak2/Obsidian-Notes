a power [[Series]] is an infinite series with a center that is a constant c. These are basically just infinite polynomials 

$$ F(x) = \sum^{\infty}_{n=0} \space\space a_n(x-c)^n = a_0 + a_1(x-c)+a_2(x-c)^2...$$
where x is a variable
