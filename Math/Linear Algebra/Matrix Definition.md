
[[Linear Algebra Bibliography]]
$$
\begin{bmatrix}
a & b \\
c & d \\
\end{bmatrix}
$$
A matrix is a 2d array of numbers that has m rows and n columns, the above matrix is a 2x2 matrix. Matrices can either be square, or not square as well, for example the matrix below is a $2*3$
$$
\begin{bmatrix}
a & b & e  \\
c & d & f \\
\end{bmatrix}
$$
