Triangular Matrices are very important for the [[Quick way to find determinant]], triangle matrices are matrices such that every value above or below the diagonal line is equal to 0:

Lower triangular 3x3 matrix:
$$\begin{bmatrix}
a & 0 & 0 \\
b & c & 0\\
d & e & f
\end{bmatrix}$$
Upper triangular 3x3 matrix:
$$\begin{bmatrix}
a & b & c \\
0 & d & e\\
0 & 0 & f
\end{bmatrix}$$
