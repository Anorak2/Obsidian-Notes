# CMDB

The CMDB is a series of tables and fields that contains all of the configuration items (C.I.) controlled by your company and their attributes and relationships.

Access to the CMDB tables and underlying data requires certain permissions such as the roles asset, itil, itil_admin, and cmdb_read

key tables: cmdb, cmdb_ci, and cmdb_rel_ci


**Configuration Items**
These can be tangible or intangible devices or applications in the CMDB such as firewalls, computers, email services, and services.
