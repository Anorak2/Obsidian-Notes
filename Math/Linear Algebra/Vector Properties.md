
[[Linear Algebra Bibliography]]
**Cauchy Schwartz Inequality**
if we have two vectors $u,v \in R^n$ then:
$$|u \cdot v| \leq ||u|| \space||v||$$
**Pythagorean Properties**
If there are two Orthogonal vectors $u,v \in R^n$ then
$$||u+v||^2=||u||^2+||v||^2$$

**Triangular Inequality**
$$||u+v|| \leq ||u|| + ||v||$$
