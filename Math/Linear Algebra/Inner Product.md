An Inner product on a function is a function such that:

$$ \langle *, *\rangle \space: \space V \times V \to R $$
that associates to each ordered pair (u,v) of vectors a real number $\langle u, v\rangle$ such that for all u, v, w in V and a scalar c we have:
- $\langle u, v \rangle = \langle v, u \rangle$
- $\langle u, v+w \rangle = \langle u, v \rangle + \langle u, w \rangle$
- $c\langle u, v \rangle = \langle cu, v \rangle$
- $\langle v, v \rangle \geq 0$ and $v = 0$ implies $\langle v, v \rangle = 0$
