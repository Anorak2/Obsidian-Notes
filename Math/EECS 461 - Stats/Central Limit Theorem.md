Given variables $X_1, X_2,...$, a sequence of iid variables with expected value $\mu_x$ and variance $\sigma_X^2$, the CDF of $Z_n$ is:

$$Z_n=(\Sigma^n_{i=1}X_i-n\mu_X)/\sqrt{n\sigma_X^2}$$ has the property
$$\lim_{n\to \infty} F_{Z_n}(z) = \Phi(z)$$
**Approximation**
$$F_{W_n}(w) \approx \Phi(\frac{w-n\mu_X}{\sqrt{n\sigma^2_X}})$$
