The transpose of a matrix is basically flipping it along a diagonal line, or more formally turning the first row into the first column, and so on.
$$
\begin{bmatrix}
a & b & c\\
d & e & f\\
g & h & i\\
\end{bmatrix}^T = 
\begin{bmatrix}
a & d & g \\
b & e & h\\
c & f & i\\
\end{bmatrix}
$$
