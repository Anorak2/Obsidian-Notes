
[[Linear Algebra Bibliography]]
Addition is defined where two [[Matrix Definition|matrices]] have the same size, and otherwise is an undefined operation. 

$$
\begin{bmatrix}
a & b \\
c & d \\
\end{bmatrix}+
\begin{bmatrix}
e & f \\
g & h \\
\end{bmatrix} = 
\begin{bmatrix}
a+e & b+f \\
c+g & d+h \\
\end{bmatrix}
$$
