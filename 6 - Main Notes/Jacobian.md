# Jacobian
The Jacobian is denoted by $Jac(g)$ or by $J(u, v)$ and it is defined by the 2x2 determinate

$$Jac(g)= \frac{\partial(x,y)}{\partial(u,v)}=det\begin{bmatrix}
\frac{\partial x}{\partial u} & \frac{\partial x}{\partial v}  \\
\frac{\partial y}{\partial u}  & \frac{\partial y}{\partial v} \\
\end{bmatrix} = \frac{\partial x}{\partial u}\frac{\partial y}{\partial v}-\frac{\partial x}{\partial v}\frac{\partial y}{\partial u}$$
